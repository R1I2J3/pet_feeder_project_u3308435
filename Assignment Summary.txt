# Pet Feeder Project

Unit: Introduction to Information Technology (8936)  
Assignment: The Solving Problem Process (Assignment 1)  
Author: Rahman Ishtiaque Joy (u3308435)  

## Project Summary
This project models a low-cost automated pet feeder for a local animal shelter. The feeder dispenses scheduled portions of dry food, verifies dispensing and consumption with sensors, logs events, and alerts staff on errors.
