Assumptions:
•	Only one type of dry kibble is used.
•	Pets are fed exactly at 08:00 and 18:00 each day.
•	The feeder has access to mains power and a basic network link for alerts.
•	Bin-level sensors and bowl-weight sensors exist and are readable.
•	Shelter staff empty/refill the hopper weekly.
•	Time source: internal RTC or network-synced clock.
